class comms_ml.traffic_models.TrafficSourceBursty(burst_counter=1, tx_len_min_bytes=10, tx_len_max_bytes=10, intra_burst_interval=(0.01, 0.07), inter_burst_interval=(0.1, 60))

A traffic source that outputs transmission times randomly that follow two schedules:

inter_burst - time between bursts intra_burst - time between packets in one burst.

inter_burst >> intra_burst.

Generates transmission times drawn from a Hawkes Process.

Hawkes builds a queue of packet arrivals and pops from that for traffic Uses max time (popped, current) so a bottleneck will still process messages in order
